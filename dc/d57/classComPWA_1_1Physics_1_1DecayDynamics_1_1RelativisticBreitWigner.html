Detailed Description

Relativistic Breit-Wigner (Breit Wigner with Blatt-Weisskopf barrier factors)

The dynamical function implemented here is taken from PDG2014 (Eq.47-22) for the one channel case.

The three required parameters are defined in this model and can be retrieved via the GetParameters() function:

Parameters
resonance_width_Width of the resonance
resonance_mass_Mass of the resonance
meson_radius_Scale of interaction range
